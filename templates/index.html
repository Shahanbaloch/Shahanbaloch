About Us

Barrett's Free House is a beloved neighbourhood pub in the heart of London. We serve the finest British fare alongside a curated selection of real ales, craft beers, wines and spirits.

Whether you're popping in for a quick pint, a leisurely Sunday roast, or a special occasion dinner — we have something for everyone.
